The Top animals in Antartica

My top 3 Choices.

Emperor Penguins

World’s largest penguin Incredible behavior (huddling, chick care) Best shots: low angle, overcast light, wide + telephoto combo


Seals (e.g., Weddell & Leopard seals)

Apex predator with dramatic expressions Often rests on ice near zodiac routes Best shots: fast shutter, eye-level from zodiac


Whales (e.g., Minke, Blue, Humpback)

Frequent breaches and fluke dives Often seen feeding close to boats Best shots: burst mode, longer focal length (300–600mm)
